Get to know me

I’m Aimi, a science communicator and visual storyteller. My interest in science communication began when I was a university student studying the structure of bark, laying the groundwork for my career as a forestry researcher. After long hours consuming articles about the periderm and phloem, I remember having difficulties truly understanding what I was reading. That is, until I came across a detailed illustration: suddenly, it all made sense. Visuals are extremely valuable to learning, especially in outreach activities such as conference presentations. I hope to become a bridge for others hoping to make sense of scientific text through the creation of accessible visuals that transcend the barriers of language and culture. I want to contribute to education in medicine and science through illustration
